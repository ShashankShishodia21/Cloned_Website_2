What Is The Future of Cinema?

Terminology

Do you feel a bit lost with all these acronyms? Yeah well that's because they've all been created very recently and yes, they are ™ and soon to be ®. Let's start with

FOC - The Future of Cinema

Which is just the abbreviation for The Future of Cinema Coin. Also it looks like film strip if you draw it in a boxy way. Easy next!

EVV™ - Evolving Virtual Volume

This is where you will be able to store all your films you've collected, and hopefully you'll also be able to decorate your EVV with art or maybe you imported a room in a show you like or you designed the structure of the EVV yourself. This space can be a simple list or a 3D batcave you made in a VR art program. You'll be able to do just about anything you want, but at its core it will just be a list of your content as if you had a video library you could flip through. The evv's can be traded or sold or given for free. You can create a store inside your EVV and sell your own digital art, and it might even connect with the metaverse or something like that.

ENFT's ™ or (Evolving Non Fungible Token)

Originally that is what the ENFAST's were called ENFT's ™ but because the trademark team just couldnt' seem to wrap their head around the fact that they were NOT nfts I moved on to ENFASTs, although I still own the copyright for ENFT. ENFAST ™ (or Evolving non fungible artistic space, token)

ENFAST ™ (or Evolving non fungible artistic space, token)

This is what the films and all of the extra media will be delivered to you in. At first it will look like whatever the production has chosen to be their artistic representation of the film. You can change that or leave it or add onto it. The ENFASTs ™ is basically the next level of the ENFT. They hold the film and all of the goodies that the creative team has made for just the holders of the ENFASTs™. The new content created specifically for the ENFAST™ holders will be stuff like the concept art and concept poster for the film, and and all art design or drawings of costume design, ,set decoration, behind the scenes, interviews with the cast and crew and of course, at the end you'll get the directors cut of the film or a cut that's different than what's released in theaters. And in addition to all of that, you will also participate in the royalties that the creative team has decided to share with their supporters. Each contract will be different and be up to the creative team ultimately to decide upon. I will suggest both a minimum of content and contribution that they should be providing.

MARQUEE

The first modular addition in both phone application and web site which is currently being created to run directly on The FOC.

Marquee will allow everyone with access to a phone or computer the ability to fund films themselves, Trade them and or sell you EVV. Imagine Marquee as the television that allows you to watch all your content. Marquee will also have a trading function where you can sell the ENFASTs ™ or ENFTs™ to another who wants to buy it and its at your discretion of the price you'd like to sell it at. You are also able to put any of the films up for rental to other Marquee users and when the film is rented you cannot watch it but the person who paid can, and you will make a % of that rental fee. If something becomes a cult classic for a generation 50 years from now and everyone wants to rent it or watch it, well, that movie could go back to the theaters and have a brand new run for a completely new generation and all sales go back to the creative team and all traffic will be tracked and this creates a brand new revenue stream for filmmakers and those that supported them early on. Cause if you still ownt hat ENFAST, well you're in for another round of royalties too. With multiple new revenue streams this completelyconcretes the stability within the currency and will continue to raise the value of everything by leaps and bounds.
